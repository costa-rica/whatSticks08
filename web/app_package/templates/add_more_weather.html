Why add more weather data?

The what sticks impact index measures how closely correlated your data are to one another. Just for adding your location WS includes 14 days of past weather history to match with your other data.

If you would like to have a more meaningful correlation it would be best to have as many days as your have been in the same city.

If you have lived in the same city since {{ oldest_date }}, you could get a more meaningful Impact Index by including weather history from 2016.

However, if you moved around a lot since then, it would be better to limit how much data you add.
